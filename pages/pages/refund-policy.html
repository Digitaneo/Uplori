Since all financial transactions occur on the official ClickFunnels platform, they are the sole party responsible for managing payments, billing, and refunds. ClickFunnels guarantees the rights of all customers and handles refund requests in accordance with their specific terms and conditions.

3. ClickFunnels Satisfaction Guarantee

ClickFunnels typically offers a satisfaction guarantee (such as a 14-day or 30-day refund period depending on the plan). If you encounter any technical errors, billing issues, or wish to request a refund, you must contact ClickFunnels Support directly to process your claim.

To ensure your request is handled promptly, please follow these steps:
